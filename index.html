Biography

I am a Research Assistant of CVIP Group in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Jianguo Zhang.

My research interest includes machine learning, deep learning and their applications in computer vision. Recently, I am interested in the topics of self-supervised learning and video understanding. I am a newbie, but I do enjoy the process of exploring.
